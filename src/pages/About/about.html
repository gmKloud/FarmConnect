About Farm Connect

Farm Connect App

Find food, farms, and farmers.

Everyone decides to eat well. We kept people in mind who do not have easy access to food. We aim to have this app help you find what food is around you. We want you to connect with your local markets and farmers to assure you have a proper meal on your plates which comes from good ol' fashioned Mother Earth.
